IEEE C37.112-2018 Inverse-Time Equations
t = TD × (A / (M^p - 1) + B)

Where:
  t  = Operating time (seconds)
  TD = Time dial setting
  M  = Multiple of pickup (I/Ipickup)
  A, B, p = Curve constants
